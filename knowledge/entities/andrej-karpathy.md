# Andrej Karpathy

AI researcher, former Tesla AI director, OpenAI founding team. Known for neural network education (Stanford CS231n, YouTube lectures) and practical AI tooling advocacy.

## Relevant Work

- Published the [LLM Wiki](../concepts/llm-wiki.md) pattern (2026-04-04) — a blueprint for LLM-maintained personal knowledge bases that Overlord adopted as its wiki architecture.
- Advocates for LLMs as "maintenance engines" — handling the bookkeeping (cross-referencing, consistency, updates) that makes humans abandon knowledge bases.
