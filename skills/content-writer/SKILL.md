# Skill: Content Writing

## Scope
Blog posts, video scripts, social media content, marketing copy, and documentation.

## Writing Process
1. **Research** — Understand the topic, audience, and competitors
2. **Outline** — Structure the piece before writing
3. **Draft** — Write the full piece
4. **Edit** — Tighten, remove fluff, check facts
5. **Format** — Optimize for the platform

## Content Types

### Blog Posts
- SEO-optimized titles and headers
- 800-2000 words for standard posts
- Include meta description, alt text for images
- Internal linking to other content on namibarden.com

### Video Scripts (Nami's Channel)
- See skills/video-pipeline/SKILL.md for full pipeline
- Meditation scripts: calm, guiding, warm tone
- YouTube descriptions: keyword-rich, timestamps, links

### Social Media
- Short, punchy, shareable
- Platform-specific formatting (threads for Twitter/X, carousels for Instagram)
- Include CTAs when appropriate

### Technical Documentation
- Clear, step-by-step instructions
- Code examples with context
- Assume the reader is competent but unfamiliar with the specifics

## Tone Guide
Refer to VOICE.md for overall style. Adapt per context:
- Nami's content: warm, serene, spiritual but grounded
- BeastMode: energetic, motivational, direct
- Technical: precise, helpful, no jargon without explanation
