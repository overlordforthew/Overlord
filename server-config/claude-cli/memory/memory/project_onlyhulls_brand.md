Official tagline: **"Where Tinder™ collides with OnlyFans™ — for boats"**

- Tinder side = AI matching (describe dream boat → ranked matches → connect)
- OnlyFans side = creator/seller listings (show your hull, direct connection, subscription tiers)
- "The OnlyFans of Boats" remains the casual descriptor
- Positioning: content/comparison site with AI matching + marketplace as secondary
- Focus niche: cruisers, liveaboards, bluewater sailboats 25ft+ (NOT fishing, dinghies, powerboats)

**Why:** Gil confirmed this 2026-03-30 as the brand direction. The playful irreverence is intentional — it's sticky and differentiates from the stale YachtWorld/Boat Trader energy.

**How to apply:** Use this tagline in hero sections, meta descriptions, social bios. Keep the tone cheeky but credible. Never sanitize the branding to be "professional" — the whole point is standing out.
