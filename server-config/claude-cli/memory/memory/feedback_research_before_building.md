---
name: Research before building
description: Gil wants thorough competitive/design research before implementing visual or UX changes
type: feedback
---

For design and UX work, do deep research before writing code. Gil explicitly said "be thorough, dig through" when asking for the OnlyHulls redesign.

**Why:** Gil wants informed design decisions, not generic implementations. He cares about competitive differentiation and wants to understand what exists in the market before building.

**How to apply:** When asked to redesign or build a new frontend feature, launch parallel research agents (competitive analysis, design patterns, market research) before planning the implementation. Present findings and get alignment on direction before touching code. This is especially true for public-facing sites where design quality matters.
